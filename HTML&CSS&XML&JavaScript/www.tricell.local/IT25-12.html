  Background
Mickey Mouse was created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks, making his debut on November 18, 1928 in the animated short Steamboat Willie. He quickly became the face of The Walt Disney Company and one of the most iconic cartoon characters in the world.

  Strengths
Mickey Mouse is brave, optimistic, and clever. He faces challenges head-on and never lets fear stop him from doing what's right. With his quick thinking and creativity, he always finds a way to solve problems, no matter how difficult they seem. Mickey's positive attitude helps him stay hopeful even in tough situations. He is also very caring and compassionate, always ready to support and help his friends whenever they need him. His kindness and loyalty make him a true friend and a beloved character around the world.

  Weaknesses
Mickey can be stubborn at times, holding firmly to his ideas even when it might be better to listen to others. Sometimes he acts before fully thinking things through, which can lead to unexpected problems. His trusting nature and occasional naivety mean he doesn't always see when someone might be trying to take advantage of him. Because of this, Mickey sometimes finds himself in tricky or challenging situations.
