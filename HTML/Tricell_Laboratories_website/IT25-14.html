  Background
Leon S. Kennedy is a former Raccoon City police officer turned elite government agent. He began his career with the R.P.D., where he was caught in the midst of the 1998 Umbrella outbreak. His survival and actions led to recruitment into a U.S. government bio-terrorism agency. Over two decades, Leon has become a highly skilled agent with extensive experience in counter-bioterrorism, hostage rescue, and high-risk diplomatic missions.
  Strengths
Leon possesses extraordinary composure under pressure, making him highly effective in unpredictable environments. His combat skills, tactical training, and investigative instincts allow him to respond decisively to threats. He is adaptive, multilingual, and consistently prioritizes mission success and the safety of others above personal risk.
  Weaknesses
Despite his strengths, Leon faces psychological scars from years in covert operations, including insomnia, emotional detachment, and chronic stress. He often shoulders responsibilities alone and may act recklessly to complete missions, sometimes straining collaboration and trust.
